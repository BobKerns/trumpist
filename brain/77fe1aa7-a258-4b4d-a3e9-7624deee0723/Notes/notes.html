Dennis Gomes, a former executive at the Golden Nugget when Steve Wynn was operating it decades ago, referenced the billionaire’s bawdy behavior in a deposition in the early 1990s.

Gomes said he “routinely received complaints from various department heads regarding Wynn’s chronic sexual harassment of female employees,” read to a court filing that summarized his testimony.

Gomes died in 2012, but his widow Barbara recalled hearing her husband say of Wynn, “I’m not his pimp.”
