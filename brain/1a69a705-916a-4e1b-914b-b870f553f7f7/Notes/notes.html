Johnson Ko

 
Securities industry veteran, Johnson Chun Shun Ko, is executive director of boutique investment firm Reorient Group, where he brought in Alibaba's Jack Ma and other investors in a $350 million deal in August. Also chairs liquid-crystal-display-maker Varitronix International, where daughter Samantha Ko is CEO. And he runs logistical and transportation-support services in Africa through Frontier Services Group.
