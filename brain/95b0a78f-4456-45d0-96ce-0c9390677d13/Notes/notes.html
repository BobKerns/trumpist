Minority Status Report

The Department of Homeland Security [DHS] reported on 2016-09-22 that it had notified 21 states affected by the Russian hack of elections systems during the 2016 election. [DHS Notice to States] The Committee should engage the NASS and interview relevant individuals from their Executive Board and/or Elections Committee to understand how each state was affected by the 2016 attack, and to better understand how they are preparing for elections in 2018 and beyond.
