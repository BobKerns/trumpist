On Thursday evening, just hours after Trump tapped him for the job, John Bolton held a call with longtime advisors, including Matthew Freedman, a Republican consultant who once advised Bolton at the Department of State and the United Nations. Freedman is currently helping manage the transition, according to a source familiar with the call.

“Freedman is a very political guy that Bolton likes,” one Republican source said. “He is overly ambitious about cleaning house.”

Freedman disputed that account, saying he was not aware of the Thursday phone call. “I can tell you there is no list,” he said.
