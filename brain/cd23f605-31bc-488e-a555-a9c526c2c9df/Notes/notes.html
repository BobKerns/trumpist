National Union for the Total Independence of Angora (U.N.I.T.A.)

The registrant assisted U.N.I.T.A. in attempting to make its case to

Congress and the American public that it is a legitimate political

organization, assisted U.N.I.T.A. with respect to U.S. media and

press strategies, and monitored the progress of legislation to aid

U.N.I.T.A. In addition, the registrant assisted the foreign

principal in making contact with U.S. media organizations and

with private and academic organizations.

$599,990.00 for the 12 month period ending December 14, 1986.

The registrant disseminated material regarding information on the

political and social infrastructure within Angola, as well as

material stating the foreign principal's positions, progress and

 

strategies.
